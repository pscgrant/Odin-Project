Chicken Stir Fry

Description

A tasty easy homemade stir fry for the weeknight.

Ingredients

  • 1lb of chicken breasts
  • Broccoli
  • Carrots
  • Red Bell Pepper
  • Green Bell Pepper
  • 2 Tablespoon of Soy sauce
  • 1 Tablespoon of Sesame sauce
  • 3/4 cup of Honey
  • 1 Tablespoon of Cornstarch
  • 1 Tablespoon of Water

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken into 1 in stripes
  2. Cook the chicken in your pan until cooked through
  3. Remove the chicken
  4. dice up all the veggies and cook with a small amount of olive oil
  5. Add back the chicken and ingredients for the sauce.
  6. Cook until the sauce has thickened and then serve
