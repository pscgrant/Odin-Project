Description

Panda Express should be exceptionally nervous right now. This sheet pan Healthy Orange Chicken and broccoli just bested its signature entr√©e. This dish is very simple to prepare and is incredibly tasty.

Ingredients

For the Crispy Baked Chicken

  • Chicken breasts
  • Panko Breadcrumbs
  • Green Onions

For the Broccoli

  • 1 medium head broccoli
  • a splash of olive oil
  • salt and pepper

For the Orange Chicken Sauce

  • Orange Zest from one orange
  • 1/2 cup of orange juice, even better if freshly squeezed
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 1 tablespoon of ginger
  • 2 cloves of garlic (2 teaspoons)
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 2 tablespoons of Cornstarch
  • a couple dashes of red pepper flakes,to taste
